Project Info

I developed a comprehensive website for a business specializing in computer sales and service. The site serves as a user-friendly platform for customers to explore a wide range of products, access repair services, and receive ongoing technical support.

Key Features:

  • Responsive Design: Fully optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Product Catalog: An intuitive layout showcasing various computers, laptops, and accessories, complete with detailed descriptions and pricing.
  • Service Booking: An integrated system for customers to request repair services and schedule appointments easily.
  • Customer Support: A dedicated section for FAQs, contact information, enhancing customer engagement.
